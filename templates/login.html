The story

Why I built this

As a student, I loved taking notes on my computer — until I hit a strict "no device policy" in middle school. Back to pen and paper. Friends were copying class notes onto their devices at home every night. I looked everywhere for a solution — nothing affordable, nothing built for this.

Note-Cloud is that tool. It uses Claude's vision AI to read handwriting, so even messy notes can make it into your notes app, email, or wherever you need them.

— Built with curiosity and a lot of notebooks.
