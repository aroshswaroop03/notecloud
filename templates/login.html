Why I built this

As a student, I've loved taking notes on my computer, solely because of the ease and compactness of digital text. I went into middle school thinking that I'd finally be able to take notes on my computer but that wasn't the case. Our school, along with many others, had a very strict "no device policy" forcing me to go back to pen and paper. Many of my friends and I had the same problem — some of them copying every note from class onto their devices once they got home. I searched through many apps but none of them were affordable or made for this purpose.

Note-Cloud is that tool. It uses Claude's vision AI to read handwriting, so even messy hieroglyphics can make it into your notes app, your email, or wherever you need them.
