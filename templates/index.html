Get the best results

A few habits make a big difference.

💡
Good lighting matters
Natural daylight or a bright lamp makes handwriting pop — low-light photos are the #1 cause of errors.
📐
Shoot straight down
Hold your phone directly above the page. The text should fill most of the frame with no angle distortion.
✏️
Dark pen on white paper
High contrast means fewer [illegible] markers. A ballpoint on blank paper is ideal.
✨
Use AI cleanup
Hit "AI cleanup" after transcribing to auto-fix spacing, grammar, and punctuation.
